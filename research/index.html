Research Projects

APPLES: Fast Distance-based Phylogenetic Placement

In this project, I developed an algorithm called APPLES, a distance-based method of phylogenetic placement that improves on ML by more than an order of magnitude in speed and memory and comes very close to ML in accuracy. APPLES has better accuracy than ML for placing on trees with thousands of species and can place on trees with a hundred thousands species where ML cannot run. Finally, APPLES can accurately identify samples without assembled sequences for the reference or the query using k-mer-based distances, a scenario that ML cannot handle.
